Meet Dr. Troy Hounshell

Your Guide to Clinical Excellence and Practice Success

"Real-world expertise matters. I'm not teaching from a textbook—I'm sharing what actually works in practice, especially in underserved communities where resources are limited and creativity is essential."

With over 25 years of hands-on experience, Dr. Troy has worked across every setting in rehabilitation—from acute care hospitals to rural outpatient clinics. As the owner and operator of Texas Therapy Pros, he's built and manages five successful therapy locations across West Texas.

Dr. Troy's unique perspective comes from real-world practice management combined with deep clinical expertise. He understands the challenges of rural healthcare, the complexity of multi-location operations, and the importance of both clinical excellence and business success.

His mission is simple: provide practical, affordable continuing education that actually improves your practice and your patients' outcomes.
